About the Author

Nwafor Sarah is the last born of Late Mr. Anthony Nwafor and Mrs. Janet Nwafor of Azuebonyi Ikwator Idembia Ohoffia in EZza South Local Government Area of Ebonyi State. She had her primary and post primary Education at Community primary School Idembia Ohoffia Ndiagu and Urban Model Secondary school Abakaliki respectively. She later proceeded to Ebonyi State College of Education Ikwo, where she did her N.C.E in Economics /English Language. Presently, she is pursuing her Degree programme in Education. Sarah is a child of God who has held several positions in the Methodist church where she belongs, especially among the Youth of the Church. She is among the Lay Preachers on note at St. Peter's church Nigeria, Awada circuit Onitsha. She is a school teacher of repute
